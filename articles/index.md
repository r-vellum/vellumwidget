# Articles

### Articles

- [Interactive widgets: a
  tour](https://r-vellum.github.io/vellumwidget/articles/interactivity.md):

  Everything vellumwidget adds on top of a static plot: hover tooltips
  (including HTML), click-select and brush, pan/zoom by
  mouse/touch/keyboard, the toolbar and export, linked views, and
  styling.

- [Linking views with
  crosstalk](https://r-vellum.github.io/vellumwidget/articles/crosstalk.md):

  Coordinate a vellumwidget widget with DT, plotly, leaflet, and
  crosstalk’s filter inputs — selection that round-trips and filters
  that hide marks, all client-side with no Shiny.

- [Very large
  scenes](https://r-vellum.github.io/vellumwidget/articles/big-data.md):

  How vellumwidget keeps a 100k-point scatter interactive: raster mode,
  a client-side spatial index, a columnar payload, and a crisp-zoom
  canvas — plus the trade-offs.

- [Using vellumwidget in
  Shiny](https://r-vellum.github.io/vellumwidget/articles/shiny.md):

  Embed a vellumwidget widget in a Shiny app and react to what the user
  does — read the selection, clicks, hovers, and brush as reactive
  inputs.
